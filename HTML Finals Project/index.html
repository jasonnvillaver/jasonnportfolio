Who is Jasonn?


A music enthusiast and a passionate guitarist, he have already performed on
various stages and events. He discovered his passion on music when he was
still in highschool. Since then, he have been improving and honing his passion
by spending time practicing and learning different guitar knowledge and skills.

A decade later, he already had different experiences and exposures to the music world;
performing and playing on different events such as YFC events, gigs, and competitions.
He even have the privilege of sharing the stage with local artists and acts,
performing on various concerts as "Front Act".

One notable appearance is the front-act of acoustic duo,
Allen G and Jasonn V for Marckenny: "Butterflies" concert and EP launch
held at Arabigo Coffee Roastery.

Experiences and appearances:

    Harana Sessions: Session Guitarist

    Youth For Christ: Music Ministry Lead Guitarist

    Arabigo Coffee Roastery Gig Artist

    Urban Coffee Studio Gig Artist

    Allen G x Jasonn V Co-writer and Guitarist

    Music genres include acoustic, alternative rock
    contemporary christian music and pop music.
